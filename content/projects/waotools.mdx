WaoTools is a website made for players in the game War and Orders to use multiple tools that will improve their gameplay. A webapp designed to make players' journey through the game War and Order less painful, giving you the ability to make plans and information to know what you need to get or do in order to grow your castle stronger.

Tech stack:
- NextJS 14
- Firebase
- Material UI v5
